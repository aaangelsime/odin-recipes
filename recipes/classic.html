The Classic Cortado

Overview

A traditional Spanish drink where espresso is “cut” with an equal amount of steamed milk. Balanced, smooth, and lets the espresso shine without being overpowering.

Ingredients

  • Espresso - two shots (~2 oz)
  • Steamed milk - equal parts to espresso (~2 oz)
  • Optional: small sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon for garnish

Steps:

  1. Grind and dose ~18 g coffee; tamp evenly.
  2. Pull espresso: ~36 g yield in 25-30 seconds.
  3. Steam milk to ~125°F / 52°C with minimal foam.
  4. Pour into espresso, aiming for a 1:1 ratio.
  5. Garnish if desired and serve immediately.
