The Gibraltar

Overview

A cortado variant served in a Gibraltar glass. Slightly less milk than a classic cortado, making it stronger in flavor.

Ingredients

  • Espresso - 2 shots (~2 oz)
  • Steamed milk - 1.5 oz (less than a Classic Cortado)
  • Optional: Small dust of cinnamon, cocoa, or latte art

Steps:

  1. Grind and dose ~18 g coffee; tamp evenly.
  2. Pull espresso into a pre-warmed Gibraltar glass: ~36 g yield in 25-30 seconds.
  3. Steam milk to ~120-125°F / 49-52°C with fine microfoam.
  4. Pour to achieve roughly a 2:1 espresso-to-milk ratio.
  5. Serve immediately while layered and glossy.
