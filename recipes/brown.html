The Brown Sugar Oatmilk Cortado

Overview

A dairy-free variation with lightly steamed oat milk and a touch of brown sugar syrup for a smooth, caramel-like sweetness.

Ingredients

  • Espresso - 2 shots (~2 oz)
  • Oatmilk - equal parts to espresso (~2 0z)
  • Brown sugar syrup - 1 to 2 teaspoons
  • Optional: dash of cinnamon or nutmeg

Steps:

  1. Grind and dose ~18 g coffee; tamp evenly.
  2. Pull espresso: ~36 g yield in 25-30 seconds.
  3. Stir brown sugar syrup into espresso until dissolved.
  4. Steam oat milk to ~120-125°F / 49-52°C (avoid overheating).
  5. Pour into espresso for a 1:1 milk-to-espresso ratio.
  6. Sprinkle spices if desired; serve.
